About Us

With many resources locked behind heafty paywalls of fourty to sixty dollars, many students have been unable to afford them, resulting in them being disadvantaged in their exams.

LazyMath was founded on the premise that these resources should be freely avaliable to everyone, not just to those which can afford them.

Our Mission

Due to the scarcity of freely avaliable VCE and university resources it has become difficult for many students, particularly those which face disadvantage, to get access to quality resources to support their education.

We aim to fill this gap by offering students free access to both CAS software, and quality notes from various VCE and university units.

We highly value feedback on how to improve the quality of our services. If you believe something could be done better, or believe the creation of a certain CAS UDF would be beneficial, feel free to contact us via email.
